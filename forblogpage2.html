NavGurukul

December, 2021

NavGurukul is a non-profit making and fully residential organisation which helps students to learn coding from the basics of programming.It does not charge in the beginning as a fee but it believes in paying forward which helps students to complete the course and then they can pay back when they are placed in any companies or startups for salaries.

Every year students from different states of India take admissions in this organisation. NavGurukul has four branches all over India. One is in Dharamshala which is for boys, two in Bangalore which are for girls and another one in Pune which is also for girls. In these campuses, there are no teachers or professors to teach the students but students of the previous batch are the mentors for the upcoming batches. The course duration is for a maximum of one year. In this one year students are taught different languages and skills of programming like JavaScript, python, react, nodeJs, web hosting, etc. And after that students are placed in different companies and startups for good salaries. To take admission in these organisations a student must have completed 10th and should be fluent in english.

Here students cook for themselves and run the whole campus on their own whether it is budget management of the campus or managing the daily schedule . This helps students to understand the daily needs in the current world and boost maturity.
